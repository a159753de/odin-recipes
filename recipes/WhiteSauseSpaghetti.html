White Sauce Spaghetti

Description

An extrodinary, epic dish called White Sauce Spaghetti has born!

Wanna make it by hand? Just follow below and get one!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 125g
  • 4slices of becon
  • 1 can mushroom
  • a helf of onion
  • 2heads of garlic
  • 1 yolk
  • a bowl of milk
  • 1 spoon of flour
  • Q.S. salt, papper, Parmesan cheese powder and italian spice

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic, slice the mushrooms and onions, and cut the bacon into about 2 cm each. ※There are 7~10 mushrooms depending on the size.
  2. Boil a pot of water and bring to a boil, add a teaspoon of salt, and take about 1/4 of the pasta package (for a package of 500g, 1/4 of the package is about two people). If you like it a little chewy, cook it for 12-15 minutes. if you like it soft and rotten, you can cook it for nearly 20 minutes.
  3. After cooking, pick it up first, you can add some olive oil and mix it to prevent it from sticking together, or put it on a plate directly.
  4. No need to add oil to the pot, just put the bacon on it and cover it all over the pot, fry on low heat, and fry until the bacon comes out of oil and minced garlic.
  5. Add onion and fry until translucent, add mushrooms and fry until cooked.
  6. Mix the fresh milk and egg yolk together, add to the pot and cook for a minute.
  7. After one minute, turn to medium-low heat, add a tablespoon of flour and mix well, then slowly stir, turn off the heat when it cooks to the consistency you like, and season with salt, black pepper, Italian spices, and coriander.
  8. Put the pasta on a plate, drizzle with sauce, sprinkle with Parmesan cheese powder, sprinkle a little Italian spice and cilantro to garnish and you're done!
